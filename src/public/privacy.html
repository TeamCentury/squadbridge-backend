SquadBridge

Nigeria's School Financial Management Platform

Last updated: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

SquadBridge ("we", "our", or "us") is a financial management platform built for Nigerian schools. We help school operators collect fees, manage payroll, and forecast cash flow. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • School information: school name, phone number, state, LGA, address, and enrolment figures
  • Identity verification: BVN (Bank Verification Number) — used only for KYC/onboarding and never stored on our servers
  • Payment data: transaction amounts, payment references, and fee collection records
  • Student data: student names, fee amounts, and payment status provided by the school operator
  • Staff payroll data: staff salary configurations entered by the school operator
  • WhatsApp messages: messages sent to our business number for support and notifications — used only to respond and send relevant financial alerts
  • Usage data: API request logs and error logs for system health monitoring

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provision and manage your school's virtual bank account (NUBAN)
  • Process fee payments and issue payment links to parents
  • Execute payroll disbursements to staff
  • Generate 30/60/90-day cash flow forecasts and P&L reports
  • Send WhatsApp notifications for payment confirmations and alerts
  • Provide AI-powered financial insights via our integrated assistant
  • Comply with Nigerian financial regulations and CBN directives

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Squad (SquadCo) — our payment processing partner, for virtual account creation and payment processing
  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) — to deliver WhatsApp notifications and receive messages
  • Anthropic — AI model provider used for financial analysis; no personally identifiable information is included in AI requests
  • Microsoft Azure — our cloud infrastructure provider (data hosted in Sweden Central)
  • Regulatory authorities where required by Nigerian law

5. WhatsApp Messaging

By providing your WhatsApp-registered phone number, you consent to receive transactional messages from SquadBridge, including payment confirmations, payroll alerts, and cash flow warnings. You may opt out at any time by sending "STOP" to our WhatsApp number or contacting us at support@squadbridge.com.

We use the Meta WhatsApp Business API. Messages are subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain financial transaction records for 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations. School profile and student data is retained for the duration of your active subscription and deleted within 90 days of account closure upon request.

7. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Access credentials are stored in Azure Key Vault. We do not store BVN numbers on our servers.

8. Your Rights

As a SquadBridge user, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about your school
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account data (subject to regulatory retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for WhatsApp communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@squadbridge.com.

9. Cookies

Our API does not use cookies. Our web dashboard may use session cookies strictly necessary for authentication. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

SquadBridge is a business platform for school operators. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. Student records (name, fee status) are submitted by authorized school staff and are processed solely for fee management purposes.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify registered schools of material changes via WhatsApp or email. Continued use of SquadBridge after changes constitutes acceptance.
